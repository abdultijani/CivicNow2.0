Privacy Policy

Introduction

CivicNow is a civic engagement platform designed to help Massachusetts residents find their elected representatives and access voter information.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and minimizing the amount of data collected from visitors.

Information We Collect

CivicNow is designed so users can access information without creating an account or submitting personal details.

  • No user registration is required.
  • We do not collect names or phone numbers.
  • We do not maintain personal user profiles.
Address Searches

When you enter an address to locate your representatives, the address is used only to determine the government districts and elected officials associated with that location.

Addresses entered into the search tool are not permanently stored.

Email Communication

If you contact CivicNow by email, we may receive your email address and any information you include in your message.

This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry.

Third-Party Links

CivicNow may include links to external websites such as government agencies or election resources.

These websites operate independently and have their own privacy policies.

Website Analytics

CivicNow may use basic analytics tools to understand general website traffic patterns and improve the user experience.

Analytics tools may collect anonymous information such as browser type, pages visited, and approximate geographic region.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect our website and systems from unauthorized access or misuse.

However, no internet service can guarantee complete security.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

CivicNow may update this Privacy Policy as the platform evolves.

Updates will be posted on this page when changes occur.

Contact
